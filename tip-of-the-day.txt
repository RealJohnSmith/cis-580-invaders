Stara Wieś [ˈstara&#160;ˈvjɛɕ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Przedbórz, within Radomsko County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 13 kilometres (8&#160;mi) south-east of Przedbórz, 39&#160;km (24&#160;mi) east of Radomsko, and 95&#160;km (59&#160;mi) south of the regional capital Łódź.
