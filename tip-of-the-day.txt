Abdulla Sodiq (Dhivehi: އަބްދުﷲ ސާދިޤް; born June 9, 1969) is the first mayor of Addu City, Maldives. He was elected mayor in February 2011 for a three-year term as mandated by the newly instated Decentralization Act. He was reelected on February 1, 2014.
