Ravno Selo (Serbian Cyrillic: Равно Село, pronounced&#160;[râːʋnoː sělo]) is a village in Serbia. It is situated in the municipality of Vrbas, in the South Bačka District, Vojvodina province. The village has a Serb ethnic majority and its population numbering 3,478 people (2002 census).
